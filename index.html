Hi, I am Lászlon Costa

Mobile communication researcher

I received a B.S. degree in computer engineering, an M.S. degree in electrical and computer engineering, and a Ph.D. degree from the Federal University of Ceará (UFC), Brazil. Currently, I hold a post-doc position at the UFC Department of Teleinformatics Engineering. Since 2014, I have been working as a researcher at Wireless Telecommunications Research Group (GTEL), Brazil, working on research projects within technical cooperation with Ericsson. In 2018, I was a guest Ph.D. candidate at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. Some topics of research interest include techniques for MIMO transceiver design, convex/integer optimization, interference management, and radio resource allocation.
